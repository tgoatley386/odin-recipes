Salad

Description

This salad is a beautiful pairing with either the lasagne or pizza - although it is also perfectly tasty by itself on a sunny lunchtime. High quality ingredients alongside extra virgin olive oil will really top off this recipe and make it one you'll come back to time and again.

Ingredients

  • 4 large vine tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 16 kalamata olives
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 85g feta cheese
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Sourdough loaf (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes, cucumber and feta into chunks.
  2. Add all ingredients together into a large bowl and mix together, ensuring everything has a covering of oil and oregano.
  3. Lightly season with salt and pepper and serve with bread if using.
